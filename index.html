"Autumn carries more gold in it's pocket than all the other seasons."



Ohio. Home of the Brutus Buckeyes. Known for the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame, and the Pro Football Hall of Fame. If you like learning about famous people, then this is the place for you.


Pennsylvania. Home to the Liberty Bell, and one of the biggest steel and coal producers in the country. You could visit the Chocolate Capital, see the first piano built in America, or even go to a Pittsburgh Steelers game.


New York. The Big Apple. The place to make all your wildest dreams come true. Broadway, the big city, questionable food. You ask, they've got it.
