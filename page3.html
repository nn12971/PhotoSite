"The city that never sleeps."



We went on a nice walk to the several overlooks of Niagra falls. The sound was deafening, but the scenery was incredible. Make sure to bring a poncho, otherwise you will get soaked.


I went on a structure that was at the base of one of the falls. You could stand underneath the falls and feel all the water pour on you. Needless to say, wear your poncho.


This picture was taken at the base of the falls. The amount of water rushing right next to me was crazy. I was very glad that I had a good coat and a poncho, because the water was splashing everywhere.


I also got to take a boat ride out in the middle of the falls. It was really windy, and the mist was so heavy, it became difficult to see. Luckily, the boat ride provided us with ponchos, otherwise I would have been completely drenched.
